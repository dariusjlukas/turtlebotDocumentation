Nvidia Jetson Nano

The Jetson Nano is a SOMSystem On Module featuring a quad-core ARM Cortex A57 processor clocked at 1.43 GHz, 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM, and a 128-core Maxwell GPU. The Jetson Nano itself is the SO-DIMMSmall Outline Dual In-line Memory Module; the form factor of the Jetson Nano card on the right. The Jetson Nano needs some kind of carrier board to operate, and we use the Jetson Nano developer kit, pictured on the left. The developer kit The developer kit includes both the carrier board and the Jetson Nano module. carrier board, pictured on the left with a Jetson Nano attached, adds 4 USB 3.0 ports, HDMI and Display Port output, a MIPIMobile Industry Processor Interface; the actual interface is CSI, or Camera Serial Interface. camera connector, power connectors, gigabit ethernet, a TTLTransistor-Transistor Logic level.
