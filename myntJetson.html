Using the Jetson Nano with a Mynt Eye Depth Camera
The Jetson Nano can be used with the Mynt Eye in the same way you would use the Mynt Eye with a computer, except that based on benchmarks Comparison of Phoronix Test Suite results from my laptop (i7-3630QM, Nvidia GT 650M, 15.6 GB DDR3L, 250 GB SSD) and the Jetson Nano. Results ranged from 1.6x faster to 21.5x faster, depending on the task. the Jetson Nano is ~5x slower than an old laptop computer, depending on the task. The Jetson Nano is also limited by its 4GB of RAM, but should be sufficient for use with the turtlebots. If you get to the point where the Jetson Nano has inadequate processing power, Nvida sells more powerful systems in the Jetson family of products, and there are a variety of options from other manufacturers.
See the Initial Setup page (step 3) for the Jetson Nano for instructions on getting the Jetson Nano working with the Mynt Eye.
